# Join

Live game lobbies are displayed on the game lobby page.

::::steps

### Step one

Click `Join Lobby` to enter the waiting room

### Step two

Change your order if desired

### Step three

Click `✔️` to indicate that you are ready to start

:::warning
Note: The game can be started at any time by the host, players not ready won't be included in the game.
:::

### Step four

If you are the game's creator, click `Start` and the game will begin
::::

:::tip[Handy Tip]
In the waiting room, click on player names to show their user info.
:::
